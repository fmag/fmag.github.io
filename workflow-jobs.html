Workflow And Jobs

You can create custom workflows to match your business processes by just dragging and dropping the appropriate applications on the workspace and connecting them together
Workflow automation eliminates manual effort freeing up critical resources for strategic work
You can create jobs out of any workflow and run them manually or as per a schedule or trigger
Jobs can also be executed from outside the platform via API invocation
You can visually monitor the status of all jobs and applications within a job and download the output and logs for analysis and troubleshooting

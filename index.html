What Problems We Solve

A. Perception that using AI needs sophisticated programming skills
• We have built our platform using the principle of lego blocks – each Flowmagic application representing a simple lego block, that can be combined with other blocks to replicate complex processes, making AI accessible to non-technical users.

B. Apprehension of AI as a blackbox
• With modular AI applications, intuitive model management and visual troubleshooting, the results are easier to understand and trust.

C. Piecemeal and isolated implementations of AI and costly integrations
• A single platform with a multitude of AI applications means that everyone in the organization uses the latest version of all applications removing any duplication.

D. Manually intensive processes that eat up your resources’ time
• Automation being a key driver of FlowMagic, the precious time of your resources will be freed up for more strategic work.

E. Operationalizing AI is a challenge – internal teams not empowered enough to manage complex AI models
• Dedicated model training and management module enables internal teams to truly take control of the AI environment.

F. Vast amount of unutilized information residing in physical documents
• Bulk processing of scanned documents can provide

G. Rigid Solutions that involve complex, time consuming and error prone deployments
• The platform is extremely flexible and deployments to production are a breeze

BENEFITS

Customizable Workflow

Flowmagic is equipped with plug and play capability. Using quick drag and drop, one can create custom workflows to address the most pressing operational functions, such as insurance agent onboarding or verifying medical invoices. Mantra Labs has pre-built over 50 AI-powered apps for its users to take advantage of. The open platform also allows insurers to create their own apps that can be seamlessly integrated.

BENEFITS

Trainable In-Built Apps

By leveraging machine learning, insurers can use FlowMagic to shift intensive operational functions into auto-pilot. The AI tool can automate the ‘classify, extract, and validate’ cycle for insurers and direct decision-ready insights straight to decision-makers. These systems can bring about at least a 70% reduction in manual processing and 30% improvement in cost-efficiencies throughout the value chain.
